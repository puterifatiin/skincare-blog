Reflection

Learning Experiences

Throughout this project, our team has gained invaluable experience in various aspects of AI and machine learning, specifically in real-time image processing and analysis. We delved into the complexities of using Support Vector Regression (SVR) and Random Forest Regression (RFR) for predicting skin conditions based on real-time webcam input. This involved meticulous model selection, parameter tuning, and continuous evaluation of performance metrics to ensure our predictions were accurate and reliable. Additionally, we sharpened our expertise in data preprocessing, feature extraction, and the integration of advanced computer vision techniques to enhance the overall accuracy and efficiency of our system.

The iterative nature of developing and refining these algorithms taught us resilience and underscored the importance of thorough experimentation in achieving optimal results. Each iteration brought new insights and challenges, pushing us to iterate on our approach and refine our models to meet the project's objectives effectively.

Overall, this project was a testament to the power of collaboration and iterative learning in AI development. It prepared us not only with technical skills but also with invaluable insights into teamwork dynamics and project management

Gratitude

We are deeply grateful to each team member for their dedication and hard work. The collaborative spirit and relentless pursuit of excellence were the driving forces behind our success. Also to our lecturer, Dr. Azura who has been dedicated in teaching us. Her commitment to teaching and mentoring provided us with the foundational knowledge and skills necessary to tackle the complexities of AI and machine learning.
