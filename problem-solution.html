Problems and Solutions

Problems

Limited Access to Dermatologist

In many areas, access to dermatologists is limited, leading to delayed diagnosis and treatment.

Time-Consuming

Booking appointments, waiting for consultations, and attending follow-up visits may be time-consuming and difficult.

High Cost

Dermatological consultations and treatments can be costly, making them inaccessible for many.

Solutions

Remote Access to Expertise

Our AI skin technology creates a platform that allows customers to upload images of their skin issues and receive professional AI analysis, ensuring that dermatological advice is available regardless of location.

Efficient Process

Implement an AI system that delivers real-time analysis and recommendations, saving patients time by minimizing the number of visits.

Affordable Consultations

Our AI skin technology provides AI-based skin analysis for a cheaper cost than traditional dermatological consultations, making skin health care more affordable.
