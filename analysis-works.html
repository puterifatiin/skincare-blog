How Skin Analysis Works?

Step 1:

Setup:

Ensure proper position by sitting in a well-lit environment and face is centered within the camera's view.

Step 2:

Face Scanning:

Open the camera and position yourself to begin the face scan.

Step 3:

Skin Analysis:

The scanned face will be analysed and processed for skin condition result.

Step 4:

Result:

The predicted percentages for each skin condition will be displayed above face.
